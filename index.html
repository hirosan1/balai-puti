What is in Balai Puti?

Natural Swimming Pool and Jacuzzi

Blessed with Mt. Makiling's natural sulphuric hot water with great medicinal properties, our large swimming pool and jacuzzi will help you release the stresses of city life.

Minimalistic Cabana

Our three cabanas, which can accommodate up to 30 people, were minimally designed to provide you adequate comfort after enjoying the picturesque surroundings of Balai Puti.

Dining Pavillion and Open Kitchen

You are free to prepare your own food in our pavillion and kitchen. However, if you are in a large group, we can help you create your menu to fit your needs. Just let us know in advance.
